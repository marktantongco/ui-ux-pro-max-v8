# Photography AI Skill v3.0

A comprehensive AI photography assistant covering six core categories to help photographers at every level.

## When to Use This Skill

- User asks about camera settings, composition rules, or lighting setups
- User needs post-processing workflows (Lightroom, Photoshop, AI tools)
- User wants genre-specific advice (portrait, landscape, street, product, etc.)
- User is building a photography business or portfolio
- User wants to integrate AI tools into their photography workflow

## Category 1: Composition

### Rule of Thirds & Beyond
- Rule of thirds grid placement for key subjects
- Golden ratio and phi grid for advanced composition
- Leading lines: converging, diagonal, S-curve, implied
- Frame within a frame: natural and architectural framing
- Negative space: using emptiness as a compositional tool

### Depth & Layering
- Foreground interest for depth creation
- Layered composition: foreground + midground + background
- Selective focus to direct attention
- Scale reference with human elements

### Advanced Techniques
- Juxtaposition and contrast (light/dark, old/new, natural/man-made)
- Pattern breaking: finding the anomaly in repetition
- Color theory in composition: complementary, analogous, triadic
- Rhythm and repetition in visual storytelling

## Category 2: Lighting

### Natural Light Mastery
- Golden hour: 30 min before sunset / after sunrise
- Blue hour: 20-30 min after sunset / before sunrise
- Overcast as natural diffuser
- Window light: direction, distance, and modification
- Open shade for even, soft illumination

### Artificial Light Setups
- **1-light setups**: Key only (Rembrandt, butterfly, split, loop, rim)
- **2-light setups**: Key + fill, key + rim, key + background
- **3+ light setups**: Key + fill + rim + background
- Light modifier hierarchy: bare > reflector > umbrella > softbox > beauty dish
- Color temperature balancing (tungsten 3200K, daylight 5500K, shade 7000K)

### Lighting Ratios
| Ratio | Mood | Use Case |
|-------|------|----------|
| 1:1 | Flat, even | Beauty, product |
| 2:1 | Subtle dimension | Portraits, corporate |
| 3:1 | Dramatic | Editorial, fashion |
| 4:1 | Very dramatic | Film noir, moody |
| 8:1 | Extreme contrast | High drama, art |

## Category 3: Post-Processing

### Lightroom Workflow (Order of Operations)
1. **Lens corrections** - Remove distortion, chromatic aberration
2. **White balance** - Set correct temperature and tint
3. **Exposure** - Get overall brightness right
4. **Tone curve** - Set contrast and tonal range
5. **HSL panel** - Selective color adjustments
6. **Local adjustments** - Brush, gradient, radial filters
7. **Sharpening & noise** - Detail panel last
8. **Export settings** - Format, resolution, color space

### AI-Enhanced Editing Tools
- **Topaz Photo AI**: Noise reduction, sharpening, upscaling
- **Luminar Neo**: AI sky replacement, portrait enhancement
- **DxO PureRAW**: AI RAW processing and denoising
- **Adobe Firefly**: Generative fill and extend
- **Evoto**: AI portrait retouching
- **ON1 NoNoise AI**: AI-powered noise reduction

### Batch Processing Strategy
1. Edit one representative image fully
2. Sync settings to similar shots
3. Auto-adjust exposure for each synced image
4. Fine-tune outliers individually
5. Export with consistent naming convention

## Category 4: Equipment

### Camera Body Selection
| Level | Sensor | ISO Range | Use Case |
|-------|--------|-----------|----------|
| Entry | APS-C | 100-25600 | Learning, hobby |
| Mid | Full-frame | 50-102400 | Professional, events |
| High | Full-frame/Medium | 50-409600 | Low-light, studio |
| Specialty | Medium format | 50-25600 | Commercial, fine art |

### Lens Recommendations by Genre
- **Portrait**: 85mm f/1.4, 50mm f/1.4, 70-200mm f/2.8
- **Landscape**: 16-35mm f/2.8, 24-70mm f/2.8, 14mm f/2.8
- **Street**: 35mm f/1.4, 28mm f/2, 50mm f/2
- **Product**: 100mm macro, 24-70mm f/2.8, 90mm tilt-shift
- **Event**: 24-70mm f/2.8, 70-200mm f/2.8, 35mm f/1.4

### Essential Accessories
- Circular polarizer (landscape, product)
- ND filters 3/6/10 stop (long exposure)
- sturdy tripod (landscape, product, architecture)
- External flash + trigger (portrait, event)
- Color checker card (commercial, product)

## Category 5: Genre-Specific

### Portrait Photography
- Posing flows: standing > sitting > leaning > action
- Connection techniques: conversation, music, prompts
- Expression coaching: real emotion vs. forced smiles
- Wardrobe guidance: texture, color, avoiding patterns

### Landscape Photography
- Planning with PhotoPills, TPE, Google Earth
- Hyperfocal distance for maximum sharpness
- Focus stacking for extreme depth of field
- Exposure bracketing for high dynamic range

### Street Photography
- Zone focusing for speed (f/8, pre-set distance)
- Candid vs. engaged approaches
- Storytelling through timing and juxtaposition
- Legal considerations by country

### Product Photography
- White background setups for e-commerce
- Lifestyle/contextual product shots
- Texture and detail close-ups
- Consistent lighting for catalog shoots

## Category 6: Business

### Pricing Models
- **Hourly**: $75-300/hr depending on experience
- **Per image**: $25-500/image depending on use
- **Day rate**: $500-5000/day depending on market
- **Package pricing**: Bundle shoot + edit + delivery
- **Licensing**: Usage-based pricing for commercial work

### Portfolio Strategy
- Show only your best 20-30 images
- Curate for your target client, not other photographers
- Consistent editing style across portfolio
- Before/after examples for commercial clients

### AI Photography Business Tools
- **ImagenAI**: Learn your editing style, batch apply
- **Aftershoot**: AI culling - selects best shots
- **Pic-Time**: Client gallery delivery
- **HoneyBook**: Booking and client management
- **ShootProof**: Proofing and sales
