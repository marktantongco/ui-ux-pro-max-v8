# SEO + GEO Content Strategy

## When to Use

- User wants content that ranks in traditional search AND gets cited by AI
- Optimizing for Google SGE, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other AI answer engines
- Building entity-based content authority
- Creating structured, cite-worthy content
- Competitive analysis for AI visibility

---

## Part 1: GEO Fundamentals

### What is GEO?
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing content so AI-powered answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE, Bing Chat) cite your content as a source.

### How AI Engines Cite Content
AI engines cite sources that:
1. **Answer directly**: Provide clear, concise answers to questions
2. **Show expertise**: Demonstrate first-hand experience and authority
3. **Use structure**: Tables, lists, step-by-step formats are preferred
4. **Are specific**: Exact numbers, names, dates, and data points
5. **Are fresh**: Recent content gets prioritized for current topics

### SEO vs. GEO Comparison
| Factor | SEO Focus | GEO Focus |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Goal | Rank in top 10 | Get cited as source |
| Content style | Long-form, comprehensive | Direct, structured, cite-worthy |
| Keywords | Exact match + variants | Entity + question-based |
| Structure | H1-H6 hierarchy | Q&A + tables + lists |
| Authority | Backlinks | Entity recognition + expertise signals |
| Freshness | Regular updates | Real-time data + recent dates |

## Part 2: AI Citation Optimization

### Citation-Worthy Content Patterns

1. **Definition Boxes**
```html
<div class="definition">
  <strong>Term:</strong> Clear, concise definition in 40-60 words
</div>
```

2. **Comparison Tables**
AI engines love tables for comparative data:
| Feature | Option A | Option B |
|---------|----------|----------|
| ... | ... | ... |

3. **Step-by-Step Instructions**
```
Step 1: [Action]
Step 2: [Action]
Step 3: [Action]
```

4. **Statistics with Sources**
"The market grew 23% in 2024 (Source: Gartner, 2024)"

5. **FAQ Sections**
Questions matching natural language queries get cited more often.

### Entity Optimization
- Use full entity names on first mention: "Search Engine Optimization (SEO)"
- Link to authoritative entity pages (Wikipedia, official docs)
- Include entity attributes: founder, date, location, specifications
- Use schema.org markup for entities

## Part 3: Content Structure for AI Visibility

### The CITE Framework
- **C**lear answers in first 150 words
- **I**nclude structured data (tables, lists, steps)
- **T**hird-party citations and data points
- **E**xpertise signals (author bio, credentials, experience)

### Page Structure Template
```markdown
# [Topic] - [Primary Keyword]

[Direct answer paragraph - 50-100 words that could be quoted by AI]

## Key Facts
| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| ... | ... |

## What is [Topic]?
[Definition + context - cite-worthy]

## How [Topic] Works
[Step-by-step explanation]

## [Topic] vs. [Alternative]
[Comparison table]

## FAQ
### [Natural language question 1]?
[Direct answer in 40-60 words]

### [Natural language question 2]?
[Direct answer]

## Expert Insight
[Author perspective with credentials]
```

## Part 4: GEO Monitoring

### Track AI Citations
- Search for your brand/content in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE
- Monitor which pages get cited and for which queries
- Track citation patterns over time

### Tools for GEO
- **Perplexity**: Search your topic, check if your content appears
- **ChatGPT**: Ask about your topic, note cited sources
- **Google SGE**: Check AI-generated answers for your keywords
- **Bing Chat**: Test query responses for citation patterns

### GEO Metrics
| Metric | How to Track | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| AI citation rate | Manual checks weekly | Increasing trend |
| Citation queries | Track which queries cite you | Expand coverage |
| Citation accuracy | Verify quoted content is correct | 100% accuracy |
| Competitor citations | Track competitor AI visibility | Outperform |

## Part 5: SEO/GEO Content Calendar

### Monthly Content Mix
- **60% SEO content**: Long-form articles targeting search keywords
- **25% GEO content**: Structured, cite-worthy content for AI engines
- **15% Fresh data**: Statistics, reports, original research (highest citation value)

### Priority Content Types for GEO
1. Original research and data → Highest citation rate
2. Comprehensive guides with structured sections → Medium-high
3. Comparison articles with tables → Medium
4. FAQ pages → Medium
5. News and updates → Low (but builds freshness)
